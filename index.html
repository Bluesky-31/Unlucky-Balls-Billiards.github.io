📖HOW TO PLAY
```
🎯 OBJECTIVE
Sink all balls before timers run out. Clear all 20 levels to win. The World Map lets you replay any completed level and aim for 3 stars.
🎱 THE BALLS
Coloured balls — sink these to score. Each hit without sinking chips them (−20% value, min 50%)
💣 Ticking bombs — sink before the timer hits zero or lose a life
😠 Angry balls — must be sunk to clear. Any hit lights a 20s fuse — sink it in time!
🗺 WORLD MAP
Tap any completed level to replay it. Stars are earned per level and saved per difficulty.
⭐ = cleared  ·  ⭐⭐ = ≤75% shots used  ·  ⭐⭐⭐ = ≤60% shots + no scratch
🕹️ CONTROLS
Desktop — mouse to aim · click or Space to fire · A / F = power · Q / R = cycle spin
Mobile — drag to aim · quick tap fires · spin pad · FIRE button
🌀 SPIN
Tap T / B / L / R on spin pad or press Q / R on desktop. Spin resets after each shot.
R key — clockwise: centre → topspin → right → backspin → left → centre
Q key — counterclockwise: centre → backspin → left → topspin → right → centre
🔥 SCORING & PERFECTION
Streak — consecutive pots without a miss · ×3 = +25% · ×5 = +50%
1-Hit sink — first-touch pot = double points (stacks with streak)
Shot budget — limited shots per level (Easy 3×, Normal 2×, Hard 1.5×, Expert 1.2× ball count). Run out = lose a life
Time bonus — clear a level quickly for extra points
Perfect bonus — clear a level without any scratches
❤️ LIVES & DIFFICULTY
Easy: 3 lives · timers ON · no sudden death · no early angry balls
Normal: 2 lives · no timers · sudden death on scratch
Hard: 1 life · sudden death · early angry balls
Expert: 1 life · more balls · less time · early angry balls
🎯 PRACTICE MODE
Empty table with one red target ball. Cue stays where it stops. Target resets each shot. No scores, no timers — just practice your shots and spin.
```
